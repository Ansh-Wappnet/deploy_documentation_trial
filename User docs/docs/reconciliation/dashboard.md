# Reconciliation Management

The **Reconciliation Dashboard** is where you monitor the execution and results of your matching processes.

![Reconciliation Dashboard](../assets/recon_dashboard.png)

## Overview Metrics
Top-level statistics provide an immediate health check of your operations:

*   **Active Jobs**: The number of reconciliation processes currently running or pending.
*   **Records Matched**: The total volume of successful matches found.
*   **Exceptions**: The number of records that failed to match and require manual attention.
*   **Success Rate**: The percentage of automated matches against total volume.

## Reconciliation Jobs
This list displays individual job executions with detailed results:

*   **Status**: E.g., *COMPLETED*, *RUNNING*.
*   **Pipeline**: The specific pipeline that generated the job.
*   **Matched/Unmatched**: Quick counts of the results.
*   **Accuracy**: The efficiency score of the specific run.
*   **Action**: Click **View Details** to dive deeper into specific jobs.
